Durga Pujo at Ekdalia Evergreen, Kolkata

Captured in Kolkata on Sptember 28, 2022 this picture represents a typical pandal (temporary shrine built during the 5-day festival dedicated to the Hindu Goddess Durga) with it's grand chandeliers and intricate decorations.

Shondhi pujo, Kolkata 2022

The transition between the 8th and the 9th day of the fortnight of Durga puja is celebrated by offering 108 lotuses and lighting 108 earthen lamps in front of the Goddess. Captured in Kolkata on October 3, 2022.
